Welcome to TriviaMaster!

Description:
TriviaMaster is an exciting and engaging trivia game designed to challenge your knowledge across various categories. Whether you're a history buff, a pop culture fanatic, a science whiz, or a sports enthusiast, TriviaMaster has something for everyone.

How to Play:
1. Choose a difficulty .
2. Answer the trivia questions within the given time limit to earn points.
3. The player with the highest score at the end of the game wins!

Features:
- Difficulty levels: Adjust the difficulty level to suit your expertise, whether you're a beginner or a trivia master.
- Timer: Test your quick thinking and knowledge under pressure with the built-in timer for each question.

Requirements:
- Compatible with Windows, macOS, and Linux operating systems.
- Minimum 2GB RAM recommended for optimal performance.
